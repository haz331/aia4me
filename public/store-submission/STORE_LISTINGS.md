## Full Description (All stores)

**AIA 4 Me — Artificial Intelligence Assistant for You**

Meet your personal AI assistant — set it up once with your own name and OpenAI API key, and it becomes *yours*. Not a generic chatbot. Your AI. Your name. Your voice.

**🎙️ Talk to it naturally**
Say your assistant's name to wake it up — just like Hey Siri or Hey Google. It listens, thinks, and talks back. Fully hands-free.

**💬 Or type when you prefer**
Fast, streaming responses in a clean dark-mode chat interface. Works beautifully on any screen.

**🔒 Your key, your data**
Your OpenAI API key is stored only on your device. Never shared with anyone. Voice data goes directly from your device to OpenAI — no middleman.

**⚡ Powered by GPT-4o**
The latest OpenAI model for sharp, accurate, conversational responses.

**🎯 Fully personalised**
Name your assistant anything — Jarvis, Nova, Max, Aria. It greets you by name and adapts its personality to feel like yours.

**🔇 Smart privacy controls**
Mic automatically stops when you leave the app or lock your screen. Toggle wake word on/off with one tap.

**What you need:**
- An OpenAI account with API access (openai.com)
- Your API key from platform.openai.com/api-keys

That's it. No subscriptions to AIA 4 Me. You pay OpenAI directly for what you use — typically fractions of a penny per conversation.
